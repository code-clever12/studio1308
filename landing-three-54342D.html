Questions

Everything You Need to Know

Common questions before your first visit — answered honestly.

How do I book an appointment?

Book online 24/7 through our Vagaro system — just click any "Book Now" button on this page. You'll get an instant confirmation. You can also call us at +1 912 800 0555 during business hours (Mon–Sat, 10am–6pm).

How do I claim the 10% new guest discount?

Simply mention "first visit discount" in your booking notes or tell us when you call. We'll apply it at checkout automatically. Valid on any single service, no minimum spend required.

What products do you use?

We use professional-grade brands including Kérastase and Olaplex — never cheap alternatives. Your hair health is the priority, not our margin. We'll recommend the right products for your specific hair type during your consultation.

Do you offer consultations before booking a service?

Yes — every appointment begins with a complimentary consultation. We take time to understand your goals, your hair history, and what results you're looking for before we start. No surprises, no guessing.

What is your cancellation policy?

We ask for at least 24 hours notice for cancellations. This allows us to offer your slot to another guest who needs it. Late cancellations or no-shows may be subject to a fee. We respect your time and ask you to respect ours.

Do you sell gift certificates?

Yes! Gift certificates are available for any amount or for a specific service. They make perfect gifts for birthdays, Mother's Day, anniversaries, or anyone who deserves to be pampered. Call or visit us to purchase one.
