Our Story
A touch of Europe in the heart of the South.

Studio 1308 was born from a simple belief: that every person who walks through our doors deserves to feel genuinely seen — and to leave looking like the best version of who they already are.

We bring European training, precision, and technique to Savannah's most discerning guests. Our team studies the crafts that elevate beauty from service into ritual — and every appointment reflects that standard.

European Craft Techniques trained in the tradition of continental excellence
Personalised Care Every service starts with a consultation. Nothing is one-size-fits-all.
Empowering Results You leave not just looking different — you leave feeling like yourself, amplified.

The Experience

Every visit follows
a ritual.

Not a transaction. From the moment you arrive, every step is designed to make you feel precisely this: that you are exactly where you should be.

I

Arrive & Settle

You're welcomed with a warm towel, a beverage, and zero rush. Your time here begins the moment you walk in — not when the service starts.

II

A Real Consultation

Not a quick nod and pick-up of tools. Your artist studies you — your face, your lifestyle, your vision — before a single move is made.

III

The Transformation

Every technique applied is a studied decision. You're not receiving a service — you're in the hands of someone who cares about the outcome as much as you do.

IV

Leave Luminous

You leave with your artist's full care instructions, a recommended maintenance timeline, and the particular confidence that comes from knowing you look extraordinary.
